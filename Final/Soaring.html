Who we are

Soaring Dragon is a PvX guild that focuses on hardcore end-game goals while maintaining the atmosphere and enjoyment of a friendly community. While we're originally from Blade and Soul, we are excited to bring our expertise and unique dynamic to Revelation Online and be one of the top guilds in the game.
